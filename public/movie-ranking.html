The Best Movies of All Time...

According to Emily

Bottoms

A perfect comedy film starring Ayo Edibiri and Rachel Sennot. Released one year ago today.

The Handmaiden

Park Chan-Wook's 2016 thriller is not only visually impressive but presents a twist so exciting you find yourself immediately desperate to rewatch the film

La La Land

Alongside Justin Hurwitz's stellar soundtrack, Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone breathe life into the greatest musical-romance of the 21st century

Some Like It Hot

Billy Wilder's hilarious comedy stars Marilyn Monroe, opposite Tony Curtis and Jack Lemmon who pose as women to escape the wrong crowd they fell in with.
